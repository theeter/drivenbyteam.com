Why Teams Get Stuck

Most teams underperform for one of three reasons. We call them The Three Gaps.

1

The Clarity Gap

“They can’t see what you see.” Your vision is trapped in your head. The team sees today’s problems. Nobody’s wrong — they just can’t see the same destination.

2

The Capability Gap

“They don’t have a path they can walk.” Your team is capable but stuck — in busywork, manual processes, or goals that feel impossible. Nobody has broken the work into steps they can actually do.

3

The Confidence Gap

“They don’t know they’re allowed to act.” Your team waits for permission because they’ve never been shown that their judgment is trusted. Every decision flows through you.

Close all three and your team drives. That’s what the Driven by Team™ tool is built to do.
